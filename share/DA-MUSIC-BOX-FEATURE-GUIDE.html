1. Mastering Bay

Finish your mix — professional mastering rack with meters, presets, and export.

Mastering Bay is the suite's dedicated mastering workstation. Load a finished stereo mix along the bottom, process through a three-unit rack, watch meters at the top, and export a mastered file ready for streaming, club, or radio.

Signal chain:
In → Bass X → DMB Match → Master X1 → Out (Optional De-Noise — before or after Master X1)
ZoneWhat you see
TopMaster meters — peak, RMS, loudness readouts
MiddleBass X · DMB Match · Master X1 rack units
BottomStereo source track — waveform, transport, clip gain
SideVU meter sidebar

Rack units

  • Bass X — Sub enhancement, drive, tone (Sub · Drive · Tone tabs)
  • DMB Match — Reference matching — amount, tone, dynamics, loudness, width
  • Master X1 — EQ, transients, compression, stereo, limiting, loudness target
  • De-Noise — Hiss reduction and click/pop catch (optional placement)

Presets: Club Ready · Streaming Clean · Warm Low End · Radio Loud · Trap 808 Punch · Podcast Clear — plus save your own with rename. Export: Save New Master with title, artist, album, ISRC, cover art, 44.1/48 kHz.

★ Built-in Auto-Tune & Vocoder

Per-channel vocal FX on the SE2 mixer — not a plugin add-on. Pitch Tune + Vocoder on every audio lane.

Pitch Tune (Auto-Tune)

Real-time pitch correction on audio lanes and Audio→MIDI lanes. Open Vocal FX or Insert FX on the mixer strip → turn Pitch Tune DSP ON.

Presets: Modern (hard snap) · Natural (transparent) · Iconic Hard · Rap Tight · R&B Melodic · Vintage

Faders: RETUNE · SPEED · FLEX · HUMAN · TRACK — plus Scale + Key tied to your song

Pitch source (pick one): From audio · Audio→MIDI on this lane · MIDI from another channel

Live pitch scope shows detected pitch and correction in real time while transport plays. Mic routes through FX live — no record-arm needed for monitoring.

Vocoder

Full vocoder engine on the same mixer channel. Turn Vocoder DSP ON for talk-box, robot, and cyber vocal character.

Presets: Robot · Transform · Traditional Funk Talk Box · Talk Box · Cyber · Warm Vox

Faders: WET · ROBOT · FORM · ATK · REL · NOISE · FOCUS · VIB

Carrier: Voice pitch (classic talk-box) OR MIDI carrier from any MIDI lane

Mixer signal flow:
Audio lane input ↓ Insert FX on mixer strip ├── Pitch Tune DSP (auto-tune) ├── Vocoder DSP (talk-box / robot) ├── Gate · EQ · Comp · Reverb · Delay ↓ Channel fader → pan → master bus

Each lane keeps its own Pitch Tune and Vocoder settings. Song-key aware. Works with Audio→MIDI conversion on the same lane.

3. SE2 Specialized Lanes

Synth Geno

  • Geno Build 1 — Live chord pads, 8-bar loop (chords, arp, bass), glide on bass & chords
  • Geno Build 2 — 4/8-bar progression loops, era/genre triggers, stack chords + melody + bass
  • Fusion / Note Flex — Sound field + Prompt field, SpaceWalk macros, Pad · Melody · Bass roll
  • Lock Groove Lead — one-click lead lane locked to Geno chords

Beat Pads

Full drum machine in SE2 — 16 pads, step sequencer, Pattern Bank (Trap, R&B, Pop, Drill, Lo-Fi), lane placements, match chords, sync slave/master/off, export WAV/MIDI.

Drum Generator

MIDI ch 10 producer kits — Pop, Trap, K-pop, R&B, Dance, Drill, Lo-Fi. Generate from chords, Bank 2 match cards, re-roll variations, 16 pad sound swaps.

Audio → MIDI

Drop audio → Melodic, Bass line, or Drums conversion. Edit on piano roll. Can feed Pitch Tune / Vocoder pitch routing.

Rhythm Edit

Chord cards above the roll — hits, beats, apply to roll, loop length. Separate from Progression+.

SE2 Chord Generator & Progression+

4/8-bar chord sketch lane vs chord timeline on instrument tracks — LOAD ALL, apply MIDI, orch hits, Groove Lead melody.

Hum Capture

Hum or sing → melody roll → piano roll. Key lock, match chords, instrument preview. SE2-local (no RVC here).

Groove Lead

WaveLeaf synth — R&B Silk, Gospel Cry, Neo Glide. Generate melody from linked chords.

Geno Ultra Synth

3 oscillators, filter, 2 LFOs, mod matrix, ARP with Chord Lock (Detect · Follow · Lock Chord).

Geno Bass Synth (Geno Bass 52)

55 factory bass sounds — Mooga, Retro, FM, analog, sub/808, funk. Wood-grain panel, C1–G3 roll.

Guitar

Loop player — R&B, Pop, Country, Funk, Blues, Rock, Latin, K-pop. Strummer, FX, quick licks, key convert.

4. Creation Station

Where beats, chords, bass, and groove are born — linked, not isolated.

Beat Lab

CH 1–16 drum pads + CH 17–32 NEW SYNTH melodic lanes. Pattern Bank, Crew Kits, Banks A–H, GRID / ROLL / SYNTH views. Export MIDI/WAV to SE2.

Groove Lab

Progression panel, genre packs, 8-bar sketch, Rhythm Edit chops, Orchid strip, CH 33–48 roles, guitar & orchestra panels, MATCH BASS.

Chord Builder

Key + mode + genre, scale pads, AUTO-GENERATE SONG, SMART VOICINGS. Export: MIDI → SYNTH, WAV → Pad, Roots → 808, song cluster.

808 Lab

Kick/bass roll + MPC drum grid. Chord Lock from Chord Builder, Groove Lab, or Beat Lab NEW SYNTH. GENERATE ROOTS, +CHORDS, BPM sync, Session Link.

Kit Generator

Procedural 16-pad kits (House, Trap, Lo-fi, R&B, Dance, Disco) — browser-only, no network. Full kit + pattern in one click.

Chord / Bass Sequencer

Step-sequenced chords (4–32 steps) with auto-locked bass — SUB/ELEC/PLUCK voices, anchor & motion patterns, bass piano roll, export WAV/MIDI/PAD.

5. AI Vocal Lab

Hum-to-MIDI · voice tools · enhancement — AI assists, you finish.

Vocal Lab (Neural Hum)

Hum or sing → melody roll → render. Key lock, 4/8 bars, mini keyboard, export WAV/MP3 → SE2, Groove Lab, Beat Lab NEW SYNTH.

Melody-to-MIDI

Record or upload monophonic audio → transcribe → piano roll. Transposition, tempo scale, quantize, confidence. Export .mid or to SE2.

Harmony Match

Hum, file, or live capture → MATCH candidates → load progression into Groove Lab → MATCH BASS.

6. AI Music Match

Vocal stem in → key detect → chord roll + bass → Groove Lab or Studio Editor 2. 100% offline.

Upload vocal/melody stem → pick analysis source (Vocals, Instruments, Bass, Full mix) → Genre + Mood → up to 6 chord candidates with auto bass → preview → one tap to Groove Lab or SE2.
