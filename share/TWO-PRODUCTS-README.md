# Two Products — Don't Mix Them Up

## StudioPilot (studiopilotmusic.com)

**What it is:** AI control for **Ableton Live**. A setup kit that connects Claude or Codex (ChatGPT) to Ableton so you can ask in plain language and get real DAW actions.

**The "box" idea:** StudioPilot is like a control box around Ableton — it arranges, builds sections, mixes, and masters inside your existing Live session using **your own sounds**.

**Key features:**
- 200+ Ableton actions (clips, tracks, MIDI, devices, arrangement, routing)
- Waveform + spectrogram analyser — AI "sees" your sound (clash scoring, key detect)
- Create · Edit · Arrange · Analyze · Mix · Master
- Setup Kit at **https://studiopilotmusic.com** (€29.99 one-time)
- Standalone one-click app — private beta, coming soon

**Share file:** `share/STUDIO-PILOT-GUIDE.html`

---

## Da Music Box (this repo / Play Store app)

**What it is:** Standalone **music production suite** — not Ableton. Own DAW (Studio Editor 2), Creation Station, Mastering Bay, AI Vocal Lab, etc.

**Key features:**
- Beat Lab, Groove Lab, Chord Builder, 808 Lab
- SE2 with built-in **auto-tune + vocoder** on mixer channels
- Mastering Bay (Bass X → DMB Match → Master X1)
- AI Music Match, Neural Hum, Melody-to-MIDI
